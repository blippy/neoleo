hcmd:p	print [r lo[,rhi]] [c clo[,chi]]
Print row. Optionally, specify row low (rlo) and row high (rhi) and col low
(clo) and col high (chi)
E.g. p 10,20
	Prints rows 10 to 20 inclusive
E.g. p 10, 20 c 2,5
	Prints rows 10 to 20, columns 2 to 5 (inclusive)
